LONDON HOUSE


London House School of English, Chiang Mai was founded in 2001. We are proud to be one of the oldest language schools in the city and have been providing English language instruction in Chiang Mai for over fifteen years. Our English School is based in northern Thailand in the picturesque mountainside town of Chiang Mai.


London House is committed to maintaining and improving standards of teaching and learning both in and out of the classroom. Our enthusiastic and dedicated English language teachers are committed to helping each student feel confident in using English in communication. We believe that the key for good communicators is all about having confidence – Confidence is all it takes.
